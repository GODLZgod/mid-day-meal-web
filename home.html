"अन्नं न परिचक्षीत । तद्व्रतम् । "

You cannot teach a child any more than you can grow a plant. The plant develops its own nature.
The child also teaches itself. But you can help it to go forward in its own way.What you can do
not of a positive nature but negative. You can take away the obstacles and knowledge comes out of
its own nature. Loosen the soil a little,so that it may come out easily. Put a hedge round it,see
that it is not kitted by anything. You can supply the growing seed with the materials for the making
up of its body,brining to it the earth, the water, the air that it wants. And there your work stops.
- Swami Vivekanand
